Our Story

From three friends to fresh, green burgers

Three Gals was born from a simple idea: burgers shouldn't be boring. Ava, Kenna, and Hannah—three childhood friends with a passion for great food—decided to revolutionize the burger scene. Tired of the same old fast food chains, they set out to create something fresh, flavorful, and uniquely their own.

What started as backyard experiments with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients evolved into a commitment to quality that rivals the best. We never compromise on freshness—our patties are made daily, our veggies are crisp and vibrant, and our signature green sauce adds that perfect herbaceous twist that sets us apart.

At Three Gals, we're not just serving burgers; we're serving memories. Each bite is a celebration of friendship, creativity, and the joy of eating well. Join us in our mission to make every meal an experience worth savoring.

"Quality you can taste, freshness you can see."
— Ava, Kenna & Hannah
